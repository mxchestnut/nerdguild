title: The Beacon Project
description: The Beacon Project trains and equips community members to
  facilitate safe, inclusive peer-support groups—both online and in person—for
  creatives, caregivers, neurodivergent individuals, and others who often feel
  unseen. It emphasizes empathy, accessibility, and belonging. Participants
  receive training in trauma-informed facilitation and community leadership,
  then launch small, independent groups supported by NPC. The Beacon Project
  expands social connection and wellness through mentorship, education, and
  shared creative expression.
