description: Hieroscope is a cultural and educational initiative that celebrates
  curiosity and lifelong learning. It provides a platform for individuals to
  explore and share the subjects they love—spanning science, art, philosophy,
  theology, history, and the humanities. Participants lead discussions, publish
  essays, and host “deep dives” into topics of fascination, creating a living
  library of collective enthusiasm. Hieroscope fosters wonder and critical
  thinking as shared values, embodying NPC’s mission to connect people through
  curiosity, creativity, and compassion.
